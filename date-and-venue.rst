Date and Venue
==============

PyCon Ph 2015 will be hosted at `Ateneo de Manila University <http://www.admu.edu.ph/>`_'s Leong Hall Auditorium on **July 4-5, 2015**.

The venue address is:

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 0
   
   * - | **Leong Hall Auditorium**
       | Ateneo de Manila
       | Katipunan Ave         
       | Quezon City           
       | 1108 Metro Manila     


A map of the venue can be found `here <https://www.google.com.ph/maps/place/Ateneo+de+Manila+University/>`_.
